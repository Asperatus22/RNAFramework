# rf-norm
The RSF Norm tool takes one (Rouskin and Zubradt methods), two (Ding method), or three (Siegfried method) RC files generated by the RF Count module, and performs normalization to obtain transcriptome-wide per-base reactivities.<br/>Reactivity scores can be computed using 4 methods:

## [1] Ding *et al*., 2014
Per-base signal is calculated as the natural log (ln) of the ratio between the raw count of RT-stops/nuclease cuts at a given position of a transcript, and the average of the ln of RT-stops/nuclease cuts along the whole transcript:<br/>
